Project Summary – Electric Vehicle (EV) Data Analysis Dashboard

This project focuses on analyzing the landscape of Electric Vehicles (EVs) — including Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and
Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) — to understand market trends, technological progress, and adoption patterns.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

Total Vehicles: Assess the overall EV market size and growth.
Average Electric Range: Measure technological advancements and efficiency in EVs.
Total BEV Vehicles (% of total): Identify and analyze the dominance of fully electric models.
Total PHEV Vehicles (% of total): Understand the market share of hybrid models.

Chart Requirements & Visualizations:

Total Vehicles by Model Year (from 2010):
Chart: Line/Area Chart
Insight: Shows EV growth trends over the years.

Total Vehicles by State:
Chart: Map Chart
Insight: Displays geographical distribution and adoption rates.

Top 10 Total Vehicles by Make:
Chart: Bar Chart
Insight: Highlights dominant EV manufacturers.

Total Vehicles by CAFV Eligibility:
Chart: Pie/Donut Chart
Insight: Shows vehicles eligible for Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle (CAFV) incentives.

Total Vehicles by Model:
Chart: Grid View
Insight: Lists top 10 EV models, analyzing consumer preferences.
